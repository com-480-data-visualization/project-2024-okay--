Our Objective

We are three final-year master's students in Neuro-X, Robotics, and Microtechnics at EPFL. Passionate about data science and its visualizations, we developed this website as part of the Data Visualization course. Our objective is to make the history and evolution of the Olympic Games accessible to everyone through engaging and visually appealing animations. This project proved to be vast, covering several important aspects, and may evolve over time.
